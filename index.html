About My DeFi Pet

My DeFi Pet is a virtual pet game that combines DeFi, collectibles and your own
personality.

My DeFi Pet is operated on Supported Network including Binance Smart Chain
and KardiaChain

Features

Collect

New monster is born every hour, and bidding lasts for
24 hours.

Breed

You can create a new generation of monster by
breeding any two monsters.

Evolve

A monster has many stages of evolution depending on
the Rarity. Evolution ensures cool new apperance and
enchanced stats.

Season and Events

Collect and maxout Legendary monster to claim
lucrative prizes.

Battle

Unique battle mode in next season & Win ultra-rare
rewards

Tradin, Ranking & Social Features

Trade on Market place to boost your collection
Compete with friends and other players
Show off your pet army
Socialise in private chat and world channel.

DPET Token

DPET token is the main in-game currency. It will be used for trading,
exchanging, improving the Pets and their special qualities, mainly in the first
phase.

Official Contract address: 0x63aCFf794521B538454a57922Cc6233417f0ebdA

Token Economics

In-game Currency

Most items in the game will be payable
using DPET tokens.

Governance

Vote for new features and settings of
the game to earn special rewards. Lock
DPET tokens for a certain time to gain
voting power

Staking

Staking part of the game by staking
DPET tokens into pools. Different pools
will yield different returns such as new
items, new eggs, or DPET token.

Play 2 Earn

Simply playing can also get you DPET
tokens via mission and social mining.
